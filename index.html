History.

Bananas are clones

That’s right, the banana you find in any supermarket, the Cavendish, is basically a clone of the others. All come from the same original plant to ensure they are equal in taste and texture.t

In the 1950s, the 'Gros Michel banana' was the star. But a disease almost wiped it out. So, the Cavendish took its place. How crazy is that?

Technically they are berries

Yes, you read it right. Bananas are basically the cousins of avocado and kiwi.

Bananas can improve your mood

They contain components that the body converts into serotonin, also known as the happiness hormone.

Facts.

The banana isn’t a tree actually is a giant grass

Although it looks like a tree, the banana is actually a grass, and its "trunk" is made of rolled leaves. It is the tallest grass in the world, reaching up to 7 meters!

The most popular fruit

Humans eat more than 100 billion a year. That's more bananas than human beings on the planet!

The shell is ideal for giving shine to your leather shoes

Simply rub the shell over your shoe and then wipe it with a cloth. Eco-friendly and practical!

Colombia, Land of Bananas.

Colombia is one of the largest exporters of bananas in the world, and many of those you enjoy at your table can come directly from our plantations in Urabá and the Caribbean coast. But not just that, when we talk about bananas and Colombia it is inevitable not to mention.

Las Bananeras Massacre

When the large plantations began to develop in the Magdalena region, the banana has not only fed our people, but it has also been an economic engine.
